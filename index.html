Our Story

At Palmyra, we are passionate about creating delicious and healthy food options for our community. Our journey began amidst the challenges of the Syrian war, driven by a simple idea: to share the authentic flavors of our cherished family recipes with busy families and individuals who want to eat well but don't have the time or energy to cook.
